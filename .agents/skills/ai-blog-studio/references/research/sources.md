## Freshness Requirements

### Data Age Priority

```
2026        ████████████ Strongly Preferred
2025        ██████████   Preferred
2024        ██████       Acceptable
2023        ████         Use with caution
Pre-2023    ██           Only for historical context
```

### Handling Dated Data

When only older data is available:
1. Note the publication date explicitly
2. Search for more recent corroborating data
3. Use hedging language: "As of 2024...", "Recent data suggests..."
4. Look for trend indicators that may still apply

## Citation Formats

### Inline Citation
```markdown
According to [Gartner's 2025 AI Report](https://gartner.com/...),
65% of enterprises have deployed AI in production environments.
```

### Block Quote with Attribution
```markdown
> "The shift to agentic AI represents the most significant
> paradigm change since the introduction of transformers."
>
> — Dr. Jane Smith, AI Research Lead at Google (2025)
```

### Statistic Callout
```markdown
> **65%** of enterprises have deployed AI in production
> — [Gartner AI Survey 2025](https://gartner.com/...)
```

### Multiple Sources
```markdown
Industry analysts project significant growth:
- Gartner estimates 65% adoption by 2026 ([source](url))
- McKinsey reports 40% productivity gains ([source](url))
- IDC predicts $500B market size by 2027 ([source](url))
```

## Verification Checklist

Before including a source:

- [ ] Is the source authoritative for this topic?
- [ ] Is the data from 2024 or later?
- [ ] Can the claim be independently verified?
- [ ] Is the URL accessible and not paywalled?
- [ ] Is attribution clear and complete?

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

### Unreliable Sources
- Anonymous blogs without credentials
- Content farms with thin content
- Sites with excessive ads/popups
- Sources with clear commercial bias

### Problematic Data
- Statistics without methodology
- Self-reported vendor data (treat with caution)
- Surveys with small sample sizes
- Data more than 3 years old for fast-moving topics

## Source Attribution Templates

### For Research Papers
```markdown
According to a 2025 study by [Author et al.](arXiv link),
[finding]. The research analyzed [methodology].
```

### For Industry Reports
```markdown
[Firm]'s [Year] [Report Name]([link]) found that [statistic].
The report surveyed [sample size/methodology].
```

### For News Articles
```markdown
As reported by [Publication](link), [fact].
[Additional context if needed].
```

### For Official Documentation
```markdown
Per the [official documentation](link), [technical fact].
```

## Building Credibility

### Strengthen Arguments With

1. **Multiple sources** - Triangulate claims with 2-3 sources
2. **Primary sources** - Link to original research, not summaries
3. **Recent data** - Prefer 2025-2026 sources
4. **Expert quotes** - Add human perspective to data
5. **Concrete numbers** - Specific beats vague

### Example Transformation

**Weak:**
"AI adoption is growing rapidly among businesses."

**Strong:**
"According to [Gartner's 2025 survey](url), 65% of enterprises have deployed AI in production—up from 47% in 2023. McKinsey's analysis suggests this will reach 80% by 2027."
