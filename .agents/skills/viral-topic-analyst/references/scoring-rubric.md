# Scoring Rubric

Score each topic from 1 to 10 across these dimensions:

## 1. Timeliness

- Is the event still fresh?
- Is there a new trigger in the last 7 days?

## 2. Spread Potential

- Could this be discussed by builders, founders, marketers, and curious general readers?

## 3. Practical Utility

- Can the article teach readers what changes for them?

## 4. Emotional Tension

- Surprise
- Contradiction
- Threat
- Opportunity

## 5. Evidence Density

- Are there enough facts, numbers, examples, or source links to support a strong article?

## 6. Search And Share Fit

- Would the topic earn both search interest and social discussion?

## Suggested weighting

- Timeliness: 25%
- Spread Potential: 20%
- Practical Utility: 20%
- Emotional Tension: 15%
- Evidence Density: 10%
- Search And Share Fit: 10%

## Tie-breakers

- Prefer the topic with the sharper thesis
- Prefer the topic that can promise one concrete takeaway
- Prefer the topic with stronger first-party evidence
