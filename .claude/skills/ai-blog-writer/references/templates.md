## Hooks That Work

### Problem-based
- "Struggling with [problem]? Here's the fix."
- "If [frustrating scenario], you're not alone."

### Curiosity-driven
- "Most developers get [thing] wrong. Here's why."
- "The secret to [outcome] that nobody talks about."

### Direct value
- "Learn [skill] in [time frame] with this guide."
- "Everything you need to know about [topic] in one post."

### Story-based
- "Last week, I spent 6 hours debugging [problem]. Here's what I learned."
- "I tried [tools] so you don't have to."
