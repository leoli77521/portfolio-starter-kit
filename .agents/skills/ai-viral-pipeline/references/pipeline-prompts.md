# Pipeline Prompts

Use these prompt shapes when the user wants the full chain:

## Full run

`Use $ai-viral-pipeline to find fresh AI topics, pick the strongest breakout angle, write the full article, and add a matching visual plan.`

## Topic first, then write

`Use $ai-viral-pipeline to find fresh AI tool topics, choose the best one for founders and developers, draft the article in English, and produce hero plus section image prompts.`

## Writing only after user-supplied topic

`Use $ai-viral-pipeline with this chosen topic and thesis, skip trend discovery, and write the final article.`

## Visuals only after article draft

`Use $ai-viral-pipeline with this finished article, skip trend discovery and topic selection, and produce the illustration plan only.`

## Fast briefing mode

`Use $ai-viral-pipeline but stop after WINNING_TOPIC and TITLE_OPTIONS.`

## Good optional constraints

- target audience
- desired article length
- preferred angle
- must-include companies or tools
- whether the article should stay neutral or more opinionated
- whether visuals should be AI-generated, stock-first, or diagram-first
